Featured Work

Complete Restroom Renovation — Father's House Church

This restroom project was completed for The Father's House Church in collaboration with StyleHome, ProTouch Construction LLC, and several volunteers from the church community. Our team designed and installed modern restroom vanities with quartz countertops, LED mirrors, and matte black fixtures. The light marble walls and dark flooring bring a clean, elegant, and modern look. It was a pleasure to be part of this meaningful project and help refresh an important space for the church.

Full Interior Work in a Newly Built Home

This beautiful new home was built from the ground up. Our team completed all the interior work except for painting. The project features bright white tones, natural wood flooring, and elegant finishes that create a warm and modern atmosphere. It was a pleasure to collaborate on this project together with BTN Homes LLC.
