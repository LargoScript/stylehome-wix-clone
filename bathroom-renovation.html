Project 48

All restrooms at Father’s House Church — women’s, men’s, and children’s — were completely redesigned and rebuilt by several members of our church, including StyleHomes. Each space was created as a custom project, combining functionality, cleanliness, and a modern aesthetic. The women’s restroom features a double sink with two height levels — one for adults and one for children — and a full-height mirror for convenience. The men’s restroom carries the same thoughtful design, with warm wood accents, black fixtures, and marble-style walls. The children’s restroom, located near the classrooms, was also designed individually to look bright, modern, and welcoming for kids. Every element was carefully planned — from easy maintenance to hidden plumbing — making each space both practical and elegant.
