About

Our Mission

At Style Homes, our first priority is you — the client. Our mission is to listen, adapt, and deliver exactly what makes you feel comfortable, inspired, and proud of your home. Every project starts with your vision, and we make it our goal to bring that vision to life with care and precision.

We specialize in decorative wall panels, wood slat cladding, and luxury Venetian plaster finishes. With more than 13 years of experience in the construction and remodeling industry, our team also provides high-quality renovation services for kitchens, bathrooms, living rooms, and custom interiors. From modern accent walls to complete remodels, we deliver results that are stylish, functional, and built to last.

What sets us apart is our ability to refresh a room in just 1–2 days with budget-friendly solutions that look premium. And beyond design, our work also increases the value of your home. Even small upgrades can make a big difference — boosting market appeal and helping your property stand out with a higher return on investment.

Many homeowners who plan to sell their property choose to work with us before listing. With minimal investments, we help raise the home's resale value significantly. Even if a realtor has already given you an estimate, the right updates can turn a standard sale into a much more profitable one.

Our mission is simple: to create cozy, modern homes where every client feels comfortable, inspired, and truly at home.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a renovation take?

Typically, a kitchen or bathroom renovation takes 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the scope of work and project complexity.

Do you help with material selection and design?

Yes, we offer design services and assist in selecting the best materials to match your budget and style.

Do I need to leave the apartment during the renovation?

It depends on the project scope. If it's only the kitchen or bathroom, it's usually possible to stay in the apartment.

Do you provide a warranty for your work?

Yes, we provide a warranty on all completed work. Details are outlined in the contract.

What is the payment schedule for the renovation?

Payment is made in stages: a deposit, progress payments, and final payment upon project completion.

Do you offer full-service (turnkey) renovations?

Yes, we provide full-service renovations — from demolition to furniture and plumbing installation.

Can I get a preliminary cost estimate?

Yes, we provide a free preliminary estimate after inspecting the site or reviewing the room layout.

Can I customize my project?

Absolutely. Every project is tailored to your preferences. We collaborate closely to ensure the final result matches your vision.
