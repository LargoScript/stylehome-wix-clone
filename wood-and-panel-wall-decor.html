Our Wood and Panel Wall Decors

Acoustic Slat Wall Panels

Perfect for recording studios, home theaters, and creative spaces. These panels are designed not only for modern style but also for function: the felt backing and precise slat spacing absorb unwanted echoes and reduce background noise. The result is a balanced, clear sound environment — ideal for music production, mixing, or simply enjoying flawless acoustics. Stylish, practical, and effective.

Modern TV Wall Design

A perfect blend of natural stone and elegant wood slats. The granite panel under the TV combined with vertical slat detailing creates a bold focal point that is both modern and timeless. This design adds depth, style, and a luxurious atmosphere to any living room — a statement piece that elevates your entire space.

Modern Bedroom Accent Walls

Bring a touch of elegance and comfort to your bedroom with slat panels combined with stone finishes. These panels not only elevate the design but also soften outside sounds, making the room more peaceful. The result is a stylish, cozy space where you can truly relax.

Cozy & Quiet Bedroom Design

Transform your bedroom into a peaceful retreat with elegant slat wall panels. Beyond their stylish, modern look, the panels help reduce outside noise, creating a calmer and quieter atmosphere. Combined with decorative stone accents and warm lighting, they add depth, comfort, and luxury to your personal space. A perfect balance of design and relaxation.

Hidden Door Wall Panels

Stylish wood panels that bring warmth and elegance to your living room while concealing a secret door. This smart design allows you to keep a clean, seamless look on the outside and a private, hidden space behind. Perfect for creating a modern accent wall with a touch of mystery and luxury.

Illuminated Wall Panels

A striking combination of modern wood panels and integrated LED lighting. These panels not only create a warm and stylish atmosphere but can also cleverly conceal a hidden door behind their seamless design. Perfect for living rooms, offices, or private spaces — a true blend of elegance, innovation, and functionality.
